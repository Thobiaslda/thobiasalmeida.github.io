Case Study: Media Relations & Performance Analysis

Developing and executing a data-driven external communications strategy for a diverse portfolio of clients in the industrial, real estate, and renewable energy sectors.

The Challenge

To manage the public reputation and media presence for multiple high-value clients simultaneously. Each client required a bespoke strategy to increase positive media coverage, enhance brand positioning on key themes like sustainability and innovation, and effectively manage emergent crises in a competitive media landscape.

My Approach

I implemented a comprehensive, proactive PR framework centered on strategic content and robust performance tracking. Key actions included:

  • Proactive Pitching & Thematic Focus: Developed and pitched stories focused on client strengths, such as GHG reduction, D&I initiatives, and technological innovation. I tracked performance against thematic goals, exceeding targets in key areas like "Sustainability" by over 550%.
  • Content Development: Created compelling press releases, articles, and media kits to support strategic narratives across digital, print, and television.
  • Data-Driven Analysis: Continuously monitored media insertions, coverage sentiment, PR value, and audience reach to measure ROI and refine campaign tactics.
  • Crisis Management: Established protocols to rapidly respond to and neutralize negative incidents, including workplace accidents and political disputes, successfully protecting client reputation.

Results & Impact
