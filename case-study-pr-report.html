Case Study: PR Performance & Crisis Management

A showcase of data-driven media relations strategy for key clients at Rede Comunicação Agency, including RHI Magnesita, CSul, and Insole.

The Challenge

To lead and execute external communications strategies for three distinct clients, each requiring a unique approach to media relations, content development, and reputation management to deliver measurable, goal-aligned outcomes.

My Approach & Key Results

For each client, I developed a proactive strategy focused on securing high-quality media placements, managing brand reputation, and handling crises swiftly. The results for our flagship client, RHI Magnesita, speak for themselves:

Key Takeaway

This project demonstrates a strong ability to manage multiple high-stakes accounts simultaneously, using data to track performance and pivot strategy while effectively handling unexpected crises to protect and enhance client reputation.
